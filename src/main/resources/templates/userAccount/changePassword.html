🔒 Password Change Instructions

  1. Request Password Change:

  2. Check Your Email:

    You will receive an email to your registered email address containing a secure password reset link . The link will be valid for a limited time. If you do not see the email, check your Spam or Junk folder.

  3. Reset Your Password:

    Click the link in the email. You will be redirected to a secure page. Enter your new password and confirm it. Make sure your new password is strong (minimum 8 characters, a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters).

  4. Password Successfully Updated:

    After submitting your new password, you will see a confirmation message. You can now log in using your new password.

Important Notes:
  • Do not share your password reset link with anyone.
  • If you did not request a password change, please ignore the email or contact support immediately.
  • Always use a strong and unique password to protect your account.
